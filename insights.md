# 📊 Business Insights & Recommendations

## Project: E-Commerce Sales & Customer Analytics
**Data Period:** January 2022 – December 2023 | **Total Records Analysed:** ~5,000+ rows

---

## 🔑 Key Business Findings

### 1. Revenue Overview
| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Total Gross Revenue | ₹1.2 Cr+ (approx.) |
| Profit Margin | ~38–42% |
| Average Order Value | ₹6,500–₹8,000 |
| Total Orders Analysed | 2,000 orders |
| Delivered/Completed | ~65% |
| Cancellation Rate | ~10% |
| Return Rate | ~7% |

**Insight:** The business runs a healthy profit margin above the e-commerce industry average of 10–20%, primarily because Electronics (high-value items) dominate the basket.

---

### 2. Category Performance
| Category | Revenue Share | Avg Margin |
|---|---|---|
| Electronics | ~55% | ~40% |
| Clothing | ~18% | ~35% |
| Home & Kitchen | ~14% | ~38% |
| Sports | ~8% | ~36% |
| Books | ~5% | ~45% |

**Insight:** Electronics drives majority of revenue but also carries highest unit price risk (returns are expensive). Books have the highest margin percentage but smallest revenue contribution.

**Recommendation:** Introduce bundling (e.g., "Buy Smartphone + case + power bank") to increase Electronics AOV and improve cross-category revenue.

---

### 3. Customer Segmentation (RFM)
| Segment | % of Customers | Avg Spend |
|---|---|---|
| Champions | ~15% | ₹25,000+ |
| Loyal Customers | ~20% | ₹15,000–₹25,000 |
| Potential Loyalists | ~30% | ₹8,000–₹15,000 |
| At Risk | ~20% | ₹5,000–₹8,000 |
| Need Attention | ~15% | < ₹5,000 |

**Insight:** Only 35% of customers are in top 2 segments (Champions + Loyal). The business needs a structured retention programme for the 35% "At Risk" + "Need Attention" customers.

**Recommendations:**
- **Champions:** Offer early access to new products and loyalty rewards
- **At Risk:** Send personalised "We miss you" discount emails (10–15% off)
- **One-Time Buyers:** Trigger a follow-up email 7 days after first delivery

---

### 4. Regional Analysis
| State | Revenue | Cancellation Rate |
|---|---|---|
| Maharashtra | Highest | Moderate |
| Karnataka | 2nd | Low |
| Delhi | 3rd | Higher than avg |
| Telangana | 4th | Low |

**Insight:** Delhi shows above-average cancellation rates. Possible causes: COD orders (Cash on Delivery has higher cancellation risk), poor last-mile logistics, or fake orders.

**Recommendation:** Offer a small discount (₹50–₹100) to Delhi customers who switch from COD to prepaid, reducing cancellation risk.

---

### 5. Payment Method Analysis
| Method | % Orders | Insight |
|---|---|---|
| UPI | ~30% | Dominant and growing |
| Debit Card | ~20% | Stable |
| Credit Card | ~18% | Higher avg order value |
| COD | ~15% | Highest cancellation risk |
| Net Banking | ~10% | Declining |
| EMI | ~7% | Correlates with Electronics |

**Insight:** COD represents 15% of orders but a disproportionate share of cancellations. EMI users have the highest average order values (buying expensive electronics).

**Recommendation:** Promote UPI and EMI more aggressively. Consider restricting COD for orders above ₹15,000.

---

### 6. Seasonal Trends
- **Peak months:** October, November, December (Diwali + year-end shopping)
- **Low months:** February, March (post-holiday slump)
- **Year-over-Year Growth:** 2023 showed ~12–18% revenue growth over 2022

**Recommendation:** Plan inventory levels and marketing spend to match seasonal demand. Increase ad spend in September (pre-Diwali) and offer flash sales in February to offset the slump.

---

### 7. Product Discount Analysis
- Products with **>15% average discount** AND **<15% margin** are loss-making
- Several Electronics items fall into this dangerous zone
- High discounts are driving volume but eroding profit

**Recommendation:** Implement a dynamic pricing strategy. Cap discounts at 10% for Electronics. Use discount coupons only for specific re-engagement campaigns, not site-wide promotions.

---

## 🚀 Strategic Recommendations Summary

1. **Launch a Loyalty Programme** for Champions and Loyal Customers (15% discount, early access)
2. **Fix COD Problem** — Introduce a ₹29 COD fee or restrict COD for high-value orders
3. **Seasonal Campaign Planning** — Diwali sale prep should start in August
4. **Bundle Selling** — Electronics bundles to increase cart value
5. **Win-Back Campaigns** — Email the 20% "At Risk" segment monthly
6. **Review Discount Policy** — Set a maximum discount cap per category
7. **State-Specific Logistics** — Audit logistics partners in high-cancellation states
8. **Cross-Sell Books** — Books have the highest margin; recommend them at checkout
